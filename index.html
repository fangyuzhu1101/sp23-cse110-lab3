Agenda of what the meeting would cover:

  • Some essential elements on webpage: rescuing cats's picture, basic info (name, age, etc.), story, like or dislike...
  • Aiming clients: nearby neighbors who could be able to pick up cats (need adopters to fill out their main information...)
  • Main goals to achieve: establish a good platform/application to help cats find their future fitting homes
  • Connect with local shelters and cooperate so using website help them better publicize their adopting needs
